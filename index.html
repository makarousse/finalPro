Living in the bautiful Cream City



Hey there, my name is Maka! I'm a person who loves taking on challenges and trying out new things. Back in 2018, I decided to embark on a new adventure as a Web Developer. As a passionate newbie, I'm always eager to contribute my creativity and learn new technologies to create amazing websites, including Shopify stores, editing photos, designing logos, and making small video projects for social media. My background is in photography, office management, and customer service, but I've found that web development is a new and exciting way to express my artistic perspective by combining some of my favorite skills..

My experience thus far has been amazing, as I have gained valuable insights into the IT landscape of this exciting field.


I'm looking forward to finding projects where I can be part of a fantastic team!
